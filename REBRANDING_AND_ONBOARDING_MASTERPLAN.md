1. **Notion:** Interactive playground (create first page immediately)

- ‚úÖ Quick win within 60 seconds
- ‚úÖ Interactive (not passive reading)
- ‚úÖ Skippable but resumable
- ‚úÖ Show value BEFORE explaining complexity
- ‚úÖ Progressive disclosure (don't overwhelm)

## REMOVING "MENSTRUAL" LANGUAGE: STRATEGY

### CURRENT PROBLEM
- Onboarding Step 2 forces life stage selection
- "Reproductive Years" description mentions "menstrual cycle tracking" explicitly
- Scares away women who:
  - Don't menstruate (perimenopause, menopause, hysterectomy, IUD, birth control)
  - Don't want to track cycles
  - Are uncomfortable with reproductive health framing

### SOLUTION: REFRAME AS "ENERGY PATTERNS"

#### **Old Language ‚Üí New Language**

| **Before (Clinical)** | **After (Inclusive)** |
|-----------------------|----------------------|
| "Menstrual cycle tracking" | "Energy pattern tracking" |
| "Track your period" | "Track your cycles (energy, sleep, mood)" |
| "Cycle-synced workouts" | "Energy-adaptive workouts" |
| "Hormone-aware" | "Biologically-informed" or "Energy-aware" |
| "Reproductive years (18-44)" | "Peak Performance Years (25-45)" |
| "Perimenopause (45-55)" | "Power Transition Years (45-55)" |
| "Menopause (55-60)" | "Strength & Wisdom Years (55-65)" |
| "Post-menopause (60+)" | "Longevity Years (65+)" |

#### **Implementation Changes**

**In Onboarding.jsx:**
- REMOVE life stage selection from initial onboarding
- Move to Settings ‚Üí Optional personalization
- Default: Show ALL features, hide none
- After 7 days, IF user hasn't set life stage, prompt: "Want personalized recommendations? Tell us your age range for better suggestions."

**In CycleTracker component:**
- Rename "Cycle Tracker" ‚Üí "Energy Cycles" (umbrella term)
- Subtext: "Track any recurring pattern: menstrual cycle, sleep cycles, energy fluctuations"
- Make period tracking OPTIONAL (checkbox during setup)
- If unchecked, show: "Track energy patterns without period data"

**In Feature Descriptions:**
- Don't lead with "menstrual"‚Äîlead with "energy"
- Example: "DualTrack helps you understand your body's energy patterns‚Äîwhether hormonal, seasonal, or stress-related."

## KEY DESIGN PRINCIPLES FOR NEW ONBOARDING

### 1. **SHOW, DON'T TELL**
- ‚ùå "DualTrack has energy tracking"
- ‚úÖ "How's your energy right now? (slider)" ‚Üí immediate smart suggestion

### 2. **QUICK WIN IN 60 SECONDS**
- User should experience value before giving personal data
- Energy check-in + smart suggestion = immediate "aha moment"

### 3. **PROGRESSIVE DISCLOSURE**
- Don't show all 15 features at once
- Reveal features as user gains context
- Example: Don't explain Gentle Mode until they track energy for 2 days

### 4. **SKIPPABLE BUT RESUMABLE**
- Power users want to explore freely
- Beginners need hand-holding
- Both should be possible with "Skip Tour" + "Resume Tour"

### 5. **ZERO SHAME**
- Don't say "You MUST complete 4 NDMs"
- Say "Your Non-Negotiables (or whatever feels doable today)"
- Gentle Mode celebrates rest, not just achievement

### 6. **CONTEXTUAL HELP**
- Tooltips appear WHEN relevant (not all at once)
- Example: Mental load warning appears when tasks > 15, not on Day 1
